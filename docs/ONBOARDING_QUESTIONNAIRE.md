# Onboarding Questionnaire

Guided by Edyn. Questions flow conversationally, one section at a time.

---

## General (Required)

### 1. Names
- **Type:** Text
- **Required:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Hey! Let's get started with the basics so we can plan your perfect day. Let's start with yours and your partner's names."

### 2. Wedding Date
- **Type:** Yes/No + Date picker
- **Required:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Great, excited to work with you [first name]! Let's get into it. What's your wedding date?"

### 3. Venue
- **Type:** Yes/No + Text (if no, location desired)
- **Required:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Awesome! And where's the big day happening?"

### 4. Guest Count Estimate
- **Type:** Yes/No + Number
- **Required:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Got it. How many guests are you thinking of inviting? This helps us plan seating, catering, and everything else."

---

## Style (Optional)

### 5. Dream Wedding Description
- **Type:** Text (3 words)
- **Optional:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Now, let's talk vibes. How would you describe your wedding style?"

### 6. Wedding Party
- **Type:** Yes/No + Number (if yes)
- **Optional:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Perfect! Do you already have a wedding party lined up?"
  - **If Yes:** "Great! How many bridesmaids and groomsmen will be in your wedding party?"
  - **If No:** "No worries, we can help you plan who to ask later!"

---

## Logistics (Required + Optional)

### 7. Vendors Already Booked
- **Type:** Multi-select dropdown (from vendor categories)
- **Required:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Time to see where you're at with vendors. Which of these have you already booked?"

### 8. Budget
- **Type:** Yes/No + Number
- **Optional:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Perfect. Knowing this helps us suggest what to focus on next. And roughly, what's your wedding budget?"
- **Follow-up:** "Got it! We'll make sure everything fits within your budget and highlight areas to save."

### 9. Pre-Wedding Events
- **Type:** Yes/No
- **Optional:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Are you planning any pre-wedding celebrations like a bridal shower or bachelor/bachelorette parties?"

### 10. Honeymoon
- **Type:** Yes/No
- **Optional:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Fun! And what about a honeymoon? Have you started planning that yet?"
  - **If No:** "No problem! We can add a honeymoon planning checklist for you."
  - **If Yes:** "Great! Let's keep track of bookings, flights, and packing reminders."

### 11. Anything Else
- **Type:** Text (open)
- **Optional:** Yes
- **Edyn:** "Now we have a great starting point. Anything else you would like me to know before I start to help you plan?"

---

## After Completion

Edyn generates the personalized task timeline based on responses, pre-populates the dashboard, and kicks off the planning journey.
