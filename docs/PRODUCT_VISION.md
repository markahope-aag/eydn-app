# Wedding Planner - Product Vision

## Bot Persona: Edyn

**Tone:** Friendly, warm, romantic, professional, calm. Fun and energetic but mature. Conversational — like a real event planner.

**Intro:** "Hi! I'm Edyn, your wedding guide. Let's make your day perfect!"

**AI Approach:** Adaptive AI for open-ended answers, structured core questions for reliability.

---

## Features

### 1. Onboarding Questionnaire

Start with a guided questionnaire to collect all relevant info before anything else:

- Partner names, wedding date, venue (confirmed or searching?)
- Estimated guest count, budget range
- Wedding style/theme preferences
- Key priorities (food, photography, music, decor, etc.)
- Any vendors already booked?
- Wedding party members

### 2. Dashboard (Homepage)

- Countdown to the big day
- Vendor summary with statuses (pending, paid in full, partial paid, etc.)
- Next upcoming tasks and overdue tasks
- Quick stats: guest count, RSVP progress, budget remaining

### 3. Task Management

- Categorized checklist with due dates
- Deadline notifications sent from the app 1 week before due dates
- Ability to attach contracts, images, or files to tasks
- Auto-generated vendor-related tasks with suggested vendor lists

### 4. Vendor Tracker

- Vendor name, category, status, point of contact (POC)
- Statuses: searching, contacted, quote received, booked, deposit paid, paid in full
- Suggested vendor lists by category (caterer, photographer, videographer, DJ, florist, etc.)
- Pre-built email templates for quote outreach

### 5. Budget Tracker

- Set overall budget and allocate by category
- Track payments, deposits, remaining balances
- Visual progress bars and spending breakdown

### 6. RSVP Tracking

- Guest list with RSVP status (pending, accepted, declined)
- Meal preference collection
- Plus-one tracking
- Integration with wedding website (The Knot, Vow'd) via API if available

### 7. Wedding Party

- Names, roles, contact info
- Day-of job assignments

### 8. Seating Chart Builder

- Drag-and-drop table/seat assignment
- Visual layout editor

### 9. Calendar Integration

- Sync deadlines and events with Google Calendar and/or Apple Calendar

### 10. Wedding Website Integration

- API integration with The Knot or Vow'd
- Sync guest list and RSVPs

### 11. Day-of Planner (Auto-Generated)

Generated 2 weeks before the wedding — print-ready document containing:

- Day-of timeline (ceremony, photos, reception, etc.)
- Packing checklist
- Day-of job assignments for wedding party
- Vendor contact sheet
- All critical info compiled in an easy-to-read format

### 12. Open Question Section

- Users can ask anything wedding-related
- Edyn responds with adaptive AI in her signature tone
- Covers etiquette, planning tips, vendor advice, timeline suggestions, etc.

---

## Notification System

- App sends notifications 1 week before task deadlines
- Triggered once wedding date is added and timeline is established

## AI Strategy

- **Structured:** Onboarding questionnaire, task generation, vendor suggestions, day-of planner compilation
- **Adaptive:** Open question section, conversational guidance, personalized recommendations
