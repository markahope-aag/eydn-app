# Wedding Planning Task Timeline

All due dates are relative to the wedding date. Edyn's messages use the persona tone defined in EDYN_PERSONA.md.

Notifications are sent from the app 1 week before each deadline.

---

## 12 Months Before

### Set Budget
- **Edyn:** "Let's start with the not-so-fun stuff…! Before anything is booked, we need a budget. Using our template, fill in line by line the budget desired for each vendor. Breaking out your total wedding budget into categories helps us keep track and stay in control without any stress! As vendors are booked, the budget can be updated."
- **Notes:** Opens the budget section with a line item for each vendor category

### Choose Wedding Date
- **Edyn:** "Pick the big day! Keep in mind the date may change if you fall in love with a venue that might not have it available."
- **Notes:** Consider the season, special dates, and when favorite people can join

### Create Guest List Draft
- **Edyn:** "Time to think about your VIPs! Start with a draft guest list—family, friends, plus-ones. You can always tweak it later. This will help with venue size and catering numbers. Fill out my prompts to get the list going."
- **Notes:** Questionnaire to help build the list

### Choose Wedding Party
- **Edyn:** "Who's going to stand by your side and cheer you on? Make a list of your bridesmaids, groomsmen, maid of honor, best man, ring bearer, and flower girl. Ask them with a little fun note or special gift!"

### Create Wedding Website
- **Edyn:** "Let's go digital! Share your wedding date, location, and schedule. Add RSVP options and your registry so guests have everything at their fingertips."
- **Sub-tasks:**
  - **Add travel info, registry, schedule** (9 months) — "Make life easy for your guests! Include hotel info, shuttle options, and the wedding day itinerary. They'll thank you!"
  - **Build registry** (10 months) — "Pick your dream gifts! Include a mix of price points and online options so everyone can join in the fun. Great places to register are any of your favorite stores or major e-commerce platforms like Amazon or Target."

### Book Venue
- **Edyn:** "Time to find your perfect backdrop! Visit venues, compare what's included, and lock it down with a deposit. Your dream day starts here. Want to see some of our top venue picks in your area?"
- **Notes:** Vendor suggestions

### Book Photographer
- **Edyn:** "Capture every kiss and dance move! Pick someone whose style you love, and consider an engagement session—it doubles as practice AND memories. Want to see some of our top picks in your area?"
- **Notes:** Vendor suggestions

### Book Videographer
- **Edyn:** "Capture every moment on film! Pick someone whose style you love. Want to see some of our top picks in your area?"
- **Notes:** Vendor suggestions

### Book Caterer
- **Edyn:** "Food = happiness! Taste test menus, discuss dietary needs, and pick your service style: buffet, plated, or family-style. Want to see some of our top picks in your area?"
- **Notes:** Vendor suggestions

### Book DJ or Band
- **Edyn:** "Time to get grooving! Choose music you love and chat with them about the vibe you want all night. Want to see some of our top picks in your area?"
- **Notes:** Vendor suggestions

### Book Officiant
- **Edyn:** "Who's going to make it official? Meet, chat about the ceremony, and confirm the legal stuff. Want to see some of our top picks in your area?"
- **Notes:** Vendor suggestions
