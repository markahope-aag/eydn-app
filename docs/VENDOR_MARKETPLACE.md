# Vendor Marketplace Documentation

The eydn vendor marketplace connects wedding vendors with engaged couples through a tiered placement system integrated directly into the wedding planning workflow.

## Overview

The vendor marketplace operates on two levels:
1. **Suggested Vendor Directory**: Curated recommendations shown to couples during planning
2. **Vendor Portal**: Self-service platform for vendors to manage their presence and purchase placements

## Vendor Portal Features

### Account Management
- **Business Profile**: Company information, description, and contact details
- **Category Selection**: Primary wedding service category (photographer, caterer, etc.)
- **Location Targeting**: Geographic areas for couple matching
- **Verification Status**: Verified badge for trusted vendors

### Placement Tiers

#### **Premium Tier ($499/month)**
- **Top placement** in vendor suggestions
- **Featured badge** on vendor listings
- **Priority in AI recommendations** when couples ask for vendor suggestions
- **Enhanced analytics** with detailed performance metrics
- **Direct messaging** capabilities with couples
- **Custom branding** options in vendor cards

#### **Featured Tier ($199/month)**
- **Second-tier placement** in vendor suggestions
- **Featured badge** on vendor listings
- **Inclusion in AI recommendations**
- **Standard analytics** with basic performance metrics
- **Profile highlighting** in search results

#### **Standard Tier ($99/month)**
- **Standard placement** in vendor suggestions
- **Basic listing** in vendor directory
- **Occasional AI recommendations** based on fit
- **Basic analytics** with view counts
- **Standard profile** appearance

### Analytics Dashboard

Vendors can track their marketplace performance through comprehensive analytics:

#### **Performance Metrics**
- **Profile Views**: Number of couples who viewed the vendor profile
- **Contact Requests**: Direct inquiries from couples
- **Recommendation Frequency**: How often AI suggests the vendor
- **Geographic Performance**: Performance by location/region
- **Category Ranking**: Position within vendor category

#### **Engagement Tracking**
- **Click-through Rate**: Profile views to contact rate
- **Response Time**: Average time to respond to inquiries
- **Conversion Rate**: Inquiries to bookings (self-reported)
- **Seasonal Trends**: Performance across wedding seasons

#### **ROI Analysis**
- **Cost Per Lead**: Placement cost divided by contact requests
- **Lead Quality Score**: Based on couple budget and wedding size
- **Booking Attribution**: Couples who report booking the vendor
- **Revenue Tracking**: Self-reported bookings and revenue

### Placement Management

#### **Checkout Process**
- **Stripe Integration**: Secure payment processing for placement purchases
- **Flexible Billing**: Monthly subscriptions with automatic renewal
- **Tier Upgrades**: Easy upgrade/downgrade between placement tiers
- **Payment History**: Complete transaction history and receipts

#### **Placement Scheduling**
- **Start Date Selection**: Choose when placement becomes active
- **Duration Management**: Set placement length (minimum 1 month)
- **Auto-renewal**: Automatic subscription renewal with opt-out
- **Pause/Resume**: Temporarily pause placements during off-seasons

## Suggested Vendor Directory

### Vendor Curation Process

#### **Application Review**
1. **Vendor Submission**: Vendors submit business information and samples
2. **Admin Review**: Platform administrators review applications
3. **Quality Assessment**: Evaluation of work quality, professionalism, and fit
4. **Approval Decision**: Accept, reject, or request additional information
5. **Directory Addition**: Approved vendors added to suggested directory

#### **Quality Standards**
- **Professional Portfolio**: High-quality work samples required
- **Customer Reviews**: Positive review history from previous clients
- **Business Verification**: Legitimate business registration and insurance
- **Communication Standards**: Professional communication and responsiveness
- **Geographic Relevance**: Service areas that match platform user base

### AI Integration

#### **Smart Recommendations**
The AI wedding assistant (eydn) integrates vendor suggestions contextually:

- **Budget-Based Matching**: Vendors suggested based on couple's budget range
- **Style Alignment**: Vendor portfolio style matched to couple preferences  
- **Location Proximity**: Geographic proximity to wedding venue
- **Availability Timing**: Vendor availability for wedding date
- **Category Priority**: Suggestions based on current planning phase

#### **Contextual Placement**
- **Task-Triggered Suggestions**: Vendors suggested when relevant tasks are created
- **Chat Integration**: Vendors mentioned naturally in AI conversations
- **Timeline Coordination**: Vendor suggestions timed with planning milestones
- **Budget Alerts**: Premium vendors suggested when budget allows
