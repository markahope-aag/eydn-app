# Target Market Analysis

## Primary Audience

### **Engaged Couples (Ages 22–40)**

**Demographics:**
- Planning their first wedding (85%) or second wedding (15%)
- Household income: $50K-$150K annually
- Tech-savvy millennials and Gen Z couples
- Located primarily in US metropolitan areas
- College-educated professionals or students

**Psychographics:**
- Value convenience and efficiency in planning
- Prefer digital solutions over traditional paper-based planning
- Want to save money by avoiding expensive wedding planners ($3K-$15K)
- Desire control and transparency in their planning process
- Appreciate AI-powered guidance and recommendations

**Behavioral Patterns:**
- Spend 6-12 months actively planning their wedding
- Research extensively online before making decisions
- Use multiple apps/tools currently (Pinterest, Google Sheets, The Knot)
- Share planning responsibilities with their partner
- Seek validation and advice from online communities

**Pain Points eydn Solves:**
- **Overwhelm**: 200+ tasks across 12 months of planning
- **Fragmentation**: Using 5-10 different tools and spreadsheets
- **Vendor Management**: Difficulty tracking 10-15 vendor relationships
- **Budget Anxiety**: Losing track of expenses and going over budget
- **Timeline Confusion**: Not knowing what to do when
- **Guest Coordination**: RSVP tracking and seating arrangements
- **Day-of Stress**: No centralized coordination plan

## Competitive Landscape

### **Direct Competitors**

#### **The Knot**
- **Strengths:** Brand recognition, vendor network, content
- **Weaknesses:** Cluttered interface, expensive for vendors, no AI
- **Pricing:** Free for couples, $300-$2000/month for vendors

#### **WeddingWire**
- **Strengths:** Vendor reviews, large directory
- **Weaknesses:** Outdated UX, limited planning tools
- **Pricing:** Free for couples, $200-$1500/month for vendors

#### **Zola**
- **Strengths:** Modern design, registry integration
- **Weaknesses:** Limited planning features, no AI guidance
- **Pricing:** Free for couples, commission on registry sales

### **Indirect Competitors**
- **Google Sheets/Excel:** Free but manual and complex
- **Trello/Asana:** General project management, not wedding-specific
- **Pinterest:** Inspiration only, no execution tools
