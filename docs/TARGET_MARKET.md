# Target Market

## Primary Audience

**Engaged Couples (Ages 22–40)**

- Planning their first or second wedding
- Looking for digital tools to simplify planning OR a way to save money to not have to hire an expensive planner
- Tech-savvy or comfortable using mobile apps
- Want to manage multiple aspects of the wedding (tasks, vendors, budget, guest list) in one place

**Characteristics:**

- Time-constrained professionals or students
- Desire organization and visual tools (checklists, timelines, seating charts)

## Secondary Audience

**Wedding Planners & Coordinators**

- Professionals managing multiple weddings at a time
- Looking for a tool to streamline communication with clients
- Can use app features to assign tasks, track vendors, and manage timelines

**Characteristics:**

- Freelancers or boutique wedding companies
- Early adopters of tech solutions to increase efficiency

## Pain Points the App Solves

- Overwhelming number of tasks and vendors
- Difficulty tracking payments and deadlines
- Miscommunication with wedding party or vendors
- Disorganized guest lists and RSVPs
- No easy way to create a day-of timeline and seating chart

## Pricing Strategy

**Freemium Model:** Basic planning tools free, premium features (vendor management, advanced budget tracking, seating chart drag-and-drop) as subscription

**Subscription Options:** Monthly or one-time lifetime purchase
